About Bug2Build

Bug2Build is an open-source, not-for-profit, student-led community that brings together students, developers, and professionals to learn, share knowledge, and collaborate on impactful projects. We aim to foster research and innovation that contribute to the technological growth and self-reliance of the nation.

Learn & Grow

Bug2Build provides real world, technical and research experience through our workshops, bootcamps, and mentorship programs. We helps students learning by collaborating, and experimenting with ideas that matter.

Build & Innovate

Turn your ideas into impactful projects. Whether it's open-source development, hackathons, or community-driven initiatives, We provides the ecosystem to create, test, and launch innovations that solve real-world problems.

Connect & Lead

Join a vibrant network of students, developers, and professionals united by curiosity and purpose. Through teamwork, leadership opportunities, and national-level collaborations, We empowers you to grow into a future-ready leader.

Events and Happenings

September 13, 2025

Online SIH Masterclass: Journey from Idea to Victory

An Online SIH Masterclass: Journey from Idea to Victory, led by an ex-SIH winner. The session will guide students through problem-solving, mentorship, and doubt-clearing, preparing them to participate effectively in internal hackathons and turn their ideas into impactful projects.

Oct 18, 2025

Digital & Cyber Awareness Workshop For Rural Government School

A Digital & Cyber Awareness Workshop for 250+ students of a rural government school, promoting digital literacy and safe internet use. The session aligns with Digital India and various state government initiatives to empower youth with essential cyber safety and technology awareness.

November 8, 2025

IIT Ropar Workshop & Exploring AI, ML & Data Science

Facilitating a group of enthusiastic students to attend One-Day Offline Workshop at IIT Ropar on AI & Machine Learning, Data Science with Power BI, and AI with ChatGPT on 9th November. Through this initiative, we aim to provide hands-on exposure to emerging technologies and support students in connecting with national-level learning opportunities.
