About Bug2Build

Bug2Build is an open source, not for profit, student-led tech and innovation community dedicated to bridging the gap between learning and real-world application for students, developers and professionals, etc.

What is Bug2Build?

Bug2Build is a student-led tech and innovation community dedicated to bridging the gap between learning and real-world application. We empower students to move from finding bugs to building solutions through hands-on workshops, hackathons, research collaborations, and open-source projects.

At Bug2Build, we believe true learning happens by doing. Our teams work across multiple domains — Technology, Research, Cybersecurity, Marketing, Events, Design, Sponsorship, and HR — to create a dynamic environment where students grow practically, not just theoretically.

Bug2Build is not just a club — it's a movement of builders, thinkers, and changemakers who believe that small ideas, when nurtured collaboratively, can create a big impact.

Our Mission

  • Foster innovation and teamwork through real projects and hackathons.
  • Build industry-ready skills by collaborating with mentors, professionals, and institutions.
  • Encourage research and social impact via rural and educational outreach programs.
  • Cultivate a culture of self-reliance, leadership, and community-driven growth.

Our Vision

  • Empowering students to innovate and build real-world tech.
  • Creating a community of learning, collaboration, and growth.
  • Bridging academia with industry through practical initiatives.
  • Supporting India's next-generation tech innovators.

What We Do

Bug2Build is a student-driven tech community dedicated to building skills, solving real problems, and growing together. We bring students from different domains under one platform where they can collaborate, learn, and innovate. Our focus is on practical learning — moving from “finding bugs” to actually “building” impactful solutions.
We create opportunities that help students gain confidence, explore new technologies, and develop leadership & teamwork skills while having fun through community engagement.

Events & Workshops

We regularly organize engaging events such as hackathons, coding challenges, tech talks, and project showcases. These events motivate students to test their skills, compete with peers, and experience real-world problem-solving.
Our workshops are designed to be hands-on and beginner-friendly. We cover trending fields like web development, cybersecurity, AI/ML, open-source tools, and many more — helping students stay updated with industry needs and practical skills.

Meet Our Founders

Bug2Build was founded by two passionate tech enthusiasts — Kastab Garai and Bhoumic Garg. Their shared vision is to build a strong and supportive student community where learning goes beyond the classroom and students can grow through real-world experience, collaboration, and innovation.

Kastab Garai

A cybersecurity learner and community leader driven by curiosity and problem-solving. Kastab focuses on building a culture of ethical hacking, research, and innovation within Bug2Build.

Bhoumic Garg

A tech-builder and operations-driven leader with experience in web development and business execution. Bhoumic ensures structured growth, partnerships, event execution, and long-term scaling for the community.

Organization & Hierarchy

  • Board Members

    Founders and senior advisors who define the vision, strategy, and governance of Bug2Build. They oversee all major decisions, partnerships, and legal compliance.

  • Executive Council

    Includes heads of departments and key coordinators responsible for daily operations, policy enforcement, and cross-team coordination.

  • Executive Bodies

    Operational teams handling the core functions of Bug2Build:

    • Tech Team: Builds and maintains the community platform, coding projects, and tech-based solutions.
    • Research Team: Drives open-source initiatives, technical research, and documentation for learning projects.
    • Events Team: Plans, organizes, and executes workshops, hackathons, and community events.
    • Marketing Team: Handles branding, social media, outreach, and communications for the community.
    • Sponsorship Team: Brings in funding, resources, and industry partnerships to support community initiatives.
    • HR Team: Manages recruitment, onboarding, member engagement, and internal coordination.
  • Collaborators & Partners

    Universities, industry experts, NGOs, and professional mentors who provide guidance, training, and collaboration opportunities for projects and events.

  • Community Members

    Students, volunteers, and participants who actively contribute to projects, attend workshops, and help grow the Bug2Build community.
